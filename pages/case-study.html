Dr.Jart+ is a globally recognized skincare brand renowned for its innovation in skincare.

Launching E-Commerce in the US

Founded by a dermatologist and an architect, Dr.Jart+ is a Korean skincare brand that is the fusion of skin, science, and art. They pioneered the BB cream category and are one of Sephora’s top-selling brands. Barrel worked with Dr. Jart+ to launch the brand’s first USA E-commerce experience on Shopify Plus. Their products were previously sold exclusively through Sephora for American customers. Dr.Jart+ has been recognized around the world for their innovation in the product design space and they have won 24 Beauty Awards, 6 Design Awards and 8 Government Awards.
